Our Moto

MISSION

To make rural masses strive for constitutional rights on their own. To strive for a society which respects laws. To make society act as a pressure group on democratic institutions .

VISION

To transform the Nation as “Country Of Rich People” by a caring and integrated system that facilitates fast track development of Rural India.

PLAN

Develop a continuous people’s participation in discharging their social responsibility towards the deprived society, with particular reference to rural India.

MAHAN BHARATH ABHIYAN

    THE MAIN FACTORS FOR HARMONIOUS EXISTENCE IN SOCIETY ARE GOOD HEALTH, FINANCIAL STABILITY AND POSITIVE RELATIONSHIPS .


    MAHAN BHARATH ABHIYAN IS A MISSION TO ACHIEVE THE ABOVE GOALS FOR EVERY INDIAN IN THE URBAN AND RURAL SPACE. 

    IT IS A CHALLENGE ENVISIONED BY COMMITTED PROFESSIONALS FROM ALL WALKS OF LIFE AS A NOBLE CAUSE FOCUSED TO BRING A TOTAL TRANSFORMATION OF RURAL INDIA. 

    THE NEED FOR REJUVENATION OF THE AGRI-ECONOMY AND RURAL EMPOWERMENT WHICH HAS BEEN MUCH NEGLECTED OVER THE YEARS BY PEOPLE OF INFLUENCE WHO HAVE MISUSED THEIR POSITION FOR THEIR OWN BENEFITS. 

    MAHAN BHARATH ABHIYAN IS PURSUING THIS VISION OF REVIVING RURAL ECONOMY SINCE 2006, WITH THE CONVICTION THAT CHARITY IS BEGGARY AND DEBT IS SUICIDAL AND ABHIYAN AIMS TO BUILD A SELF- SUPPORTING SOCIO ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT MODEL. 
    OUR ENDEAVOUR IS TO MAKE EVERY RURAL INDIAN ACHIEVE FINANCIAL STABILITY AND TO ENJOY A BETTER QUALITY LIFE. 


    HENCE IT IS AN APPEAL TO ONE AND ALL TO JOIN THIS NOBLE CAUSE IN WHATEVER CAPACITY YOU LIKE TO CHOOSE, SO FRIENDS LET US ALL JOIN TOGETHER NOW TO MAKE OUR COUNTRY ONCE AGAIN ‘MERA BHARATH MAHAN’ INSTEAD OF FUTURE GENERATIONS ASKING ‘MERA BHARATH KAHAN?’. 

    “MAHAN BHARATH”
